Current interests

Streamlining banking/payment services for the underserved - Being poor in America is expensive. It costs low-income Americans not only their money, but also an inordinate amount of their time to survive in America. Most underbanked spend countless hours waiting in line after line just to pay bills, withdraw cash, and other basic tasks. Saving Americans time = giving them more opportunities to pull themselves out of poverty.

Renewing public interest in space - It's a matter of when, not if we'll become a multi-planetary civilization. I just want to see it happen sooner.

Advanced voice interfaces - With the breakneck progress being made on both voice synthesis tools and AI tools like OpenAI's GPT-3, the next steps are possible.
